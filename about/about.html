About Us - Doctor Binary

We're Doctor Binary, a small team of three who are passionate about both software engineering and mental health. As psychology enthusiasts, we want to use technology to make a positive difference. That's why we've created Coffee Break, a project close to our hearts. We introduce Milo, the friendliest mental health companion for students, designed to offer support when it's needed the most. Together, we aim to make the world a better place -- one step at a time.
