Resources

This page offers a curated selection of books, videos, and tools designed to support your mental well-being. Whether you're looking for a calming read, practical advice or immediate assistance, everything you need is a scroll away.
Books
Feeling Great by David D Burns, MD
This is a classic self-help book that provides practical exercises like Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) and journaling prompts to help you identify and challenge negative thoughts.
The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People by Stephen R. Covey
This timeless classic explores deeper principles of character and personal leadership. Discover more balance, purpose, and success in your life by embracing these powerful habits.
Atomic Habits by James Clear
James Clear explores the habit behind habit formation and gives a practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones. Learn how to make small, consistent improvements that compound over time.
The Happiness Trap by Russ Harris, MD
Russ Harris introduces Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) to help embrace your thoughts and feelings without judgement and focus on living a meaningful and values-driven life.
